Driving Lessons with Patience, Care & Real Results

Preet is a fully RSA Approved Driving Instructor based in D18, serving Dublin 18 and all surrounding areas with professional manual driving lessons tailored to every student.

What do I need before my first lesson?
You'll need a valid Irish Learner Permit (category B). You first need to pass the Driver Theory Test, then apply at your local NDLS office for your permit. Bring your permit to every lesson — Preet will verify it before you drive.
What areas does Preet cover?
Preet is based in D18 and covers Dublin 18 and all surrounding areas. Pickup from your home, college, or workplace is typically included — contact Preet directly to confirm your specific area.
What is the EDT and do I need it?
Yes — the EDT (Essential Driver Training) is mandatory for all first-time Irish driving licence applicants. It consists of 12 structured lessons covering key skills. Preet is fully certified to deliver all 12 EDT topics and logs your progress with the RSA after every session.
Can I use Preet's car on my test day?
Yes! Car hire for your driving test is available. Taking your test in a car you've trained in is one of the biggest confidence boosters going. Contact Preet to arrange test-day car hire and a warmup lesson beforehand.
How many lessons will I need to pass?
This varies for every student. After an initial assessment lesson, Preet will give you an honest estimate based on your current ability. There's never any pressure to book more lessons than you genuinely need.
What is Preet's cancellation policy?
Please give at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule a lesson. Cancellations with less notice may be charged. Genuine emergencies are always handled with understanding — just get in touch as soon as possible.
